Sikh Ceremony

Things to keep in mind regarding attire for the Sikh ceremony. Please visit SikhiWiki for more detailed information.

  • Comfort - traditionally, seating is on the floor. Please dress comfortably, as the ceremony will last approxiamately 45 minutes.
  • Socks - shoes must be removed before entering the temple. Bare feet are allowed. If you prefer, non-"HOLY" socks are also allowed.
