The Proposal

Initially, my plan was to go to the highest point in Prospect Park to propose, but Roli foiled that idea by not wanting to leave the house. I finally convinced her to go out to get vegetables at the farmers market a block away, so we could have fresh produce for dinner. It worked!!! On the way back home, we walked through Washington Park, also known as Stone Park. I found a nice secluded area with a bench and signed my life away!!!
